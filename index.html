Founder Building

Founder Initiatives
— zero100

We don't just co-build startups — we build founders.

Zero100 is a founder development program created and led by Wilt Venture Builder, built on the belief that the journey from 0 to 100 is what truly defines a resilient founder.

Born from a desire to address the "missing middle" of Korea's startup ecosystem — where many startups stop at 10 while few reach 100 — Zero100 empowers founders to build with clarity, collaboration, and global standards in mind.
